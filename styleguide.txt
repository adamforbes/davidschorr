ADAM FORBES
STYLE GUIDE FOR HTML/CSS/JS

HTML
All elements with siblings that aren't leaves get a line break between them
Example:
<div>

  <div>
    <div></div>
    <div></div>
    <div></div>
  </div>

  <div>
    <div>
    </div>
  </div>

  <div>
    
    <div>
      <div>
      </div>
    </div>
    
    <div>
    </div>

  <div>

</div>
